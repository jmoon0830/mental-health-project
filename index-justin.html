Inspiration

Mental health in the workplace is a serious and important topic. According to a Harvard study, almost 20% of American employees from ages 15-54 experience symptoms of mental health disorders. In addition, these employees may not even seek help because they feel it could jeaopardize their jobs. As their is a connection between mental health and productivity in the workplace, this website pulls datasets from 2014 and 2016 to find signficances in employees' responses to questions regarding mental health in the workplace.
